## Hacker News — Show HN

**Title:** Show HN: Lode – Spot price alerts and portfolio tracker for precious metals

**First comment:**
Hey HN — I stack gold and silver and got tired of juggling tabs to check prices and manually watch for buy targets.

Lode does a few things:

- Live spot prices for gold, silver, platinum, and palladium (updated every 15 minutes via a cron job)
- Portfolio tracker — log your oz, see value and P&L at current spot
- Email price alerts when a metal hits your target price
- Coin melt value calculator (Eagles, Maple Leafs, pre-65 junk silver, scrap)
- Weekly portfolio digest email (Pro only)

Stack: Next.js 14 App Router, Prisma, Postgres on Neon, deployed on Vercel. Prices from a metals data API. Auth via NextAuth. Payments via Stripe.

# Facebook Groups Playbook — lode.rocks

### Rule #1: Replies only. Never post standalone promos. Find someone asking a real question, answer it genuinely, and mention Lode naturally.
### Rule #2: Don't open with the name. Lead with the answer, drop the name at the end.
### Rule #3: One comment per post, max. Reply to comments within that post freely.
### Rule #4: Max 3–4 comments per day across all groups. FB flags promotional-feeling accounts quickly.

## How to find the right posts

In each group, use the search bar (inside the group, not global Facebook) and run:

- `track my stack`
- `price alert`
- `spot price`
- `melt value`
- `portfolio`
- `how do you know when to buy`

Sort by **Recent**. Look for posts from the last 7 days with active comment threads.

Also watch the group feed for:
- "What app do you use?" posts — extremely high-value
- "Gold just hit $X!" posts — perfect for the alert angle
- Haul / stack photo posts — portfolio tracker angle fits naturally
- "What's the melt value of [coin]?" posts — direct melt calculator fit
